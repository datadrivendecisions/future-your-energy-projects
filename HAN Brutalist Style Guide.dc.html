05Logo Usage
Keep clear space around the logo equal to at least the height of the springboard mark. Place it only on Raw Paper or White — never inside a pink or black panel. Minimum width 80px. Never rotate, skew or recolor the mark itself — skewing is reserved for the pink number tags only.
06Do & Don't
Do
  • Use hard, offset drop shadows with zero blur
  • Keep every corner square — no border-radius, anywhere
  • Pair heavy uppercase display type with plain mono for data
  • Let pink appear in exactly one role: the section number tag
Don't
  • Soften borders or shadows to look "friendlier"
  • Introduce a third accent color
  • Center-align body copy — keep it left or right, never centered
  • Shrink the outer frame below 4px; it's structural, not decorative
