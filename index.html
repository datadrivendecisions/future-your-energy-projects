Project Charters — Minor Data Driven Decision Making in Business
Two project charters for the 3DMiB challenge, set by Jeroen Veen (Researcher HAN, Future Energy Research Group). P1 and P2 structure and unlock the FyE neighborhood data lake for the Elsweide case; P3 explores the behavioral obstacles to a Positive Energy District through an AI-persona simulation.
P1 + P2

From Data Lake to Queryable Knowledge

Ontology and agent-ready access: structuring the Elsweide data lake into a defensible domain ontology, then making it queryable in plain language with source attribution. Team of 4.
